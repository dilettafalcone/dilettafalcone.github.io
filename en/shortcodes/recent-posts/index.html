Recent Posts

Render recent posts list

Usage 

{{< recent-posts sortby="" limit="" >}}

Paramters:

  • sortby: publishDate or lastMod
  • limit: positive integer, 0 means unlimited

Examples 

{{< recent-posts sortby="lastMod" limit=5 >}}
